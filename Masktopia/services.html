Additional Information

  • Qualities of a good face mask

    The quality of the face mask you are wearing is important because it lessens the danger of contracting covid-19. If your facemask is of bad quality, you could be putting yourself in danger without knowing it.

    The highest quality face masks use at least two layers of tightly woven cotton fabric. Some face masks have an additional filter to provide extra protection. The filter in the mask needs to be dense enough to block small particles, while also being breathable and comfortable for the user.

    You must be able to breathe comfortably while wearing your facemask. In other words, you should not need to remove your facemask in order to take a breath of air. If you have a reusable mask, you will need to wash your face mask after every trip out of the home. If buying your mask online, make sure to look at reviews that comment on the masks wear and tear.

  • Where to Buy Quality Face Masks

    There are several online places to buy facemasks. Make sure to read the description and check the reviews to see if the mask is of good quality.

    Some of the best places include nowinstock.net, Wallgreens.com, and Amazon.com.

  • How A Face Mask Works/Why You Should Wear One

    Many people can have the corona virus yet not exhibit any symptoms. This is why wearing a mask even if you do not feel any sickness symptoms is necessary.

    According to this 2013 study, masks led to more than a threefold reduction in the amount of virus that people sprayed into the air. Consequently, the amount of people affected by the virus went down.

    If a person sneezes and coughs, their respiratory droplets could land on people and surfaces. When you wear a mask, the droplets would hit your mask instead of your nose and mouth, therefore protecting you.

  • How Do I Properly Put On A Face Mask

    Clean your hands with soap and water or hand sanitizer before touching the mask. Remove a mask from the box and make sure there are no obvious tears or holes in either side of the mask. Determine which side of the mask is the top. The side of the mask that has a stiff bendable edge is the top and is meant to mold to the shape of your nose.

    Determine which side of the mask is the front. The colored side of the mask is usually the front and should face away from you, while the white side touches your face. Hold the mask by the ear loops. Place a loop around each ear. Mold or pinch the stiff edge to the shape of your nose. Pull the bottom of the mask over your mouth and chin.

  • How Do I Properly Remove A Face Mask

    Avoid touching the front of the mask. The front of the mask is contaminated. Only touch the ear loops/ties/band. Hold both of the ear loops and gently lift and remove the mask. Throw the mask in the trash. Clean your hands with soap and water or hand sanitizer.

  • Who Should Not Wear Face Masks

    Children under two years old, People with breathing problems, People who can't remove the mask without assistance, such as those who are incapacitated or disabled.

  • Homemade masks

    NPR reported that the best material to make a face mask is to use cotton T-shirt material or tea towels. It is best to wear surgical/medical face masks whenever you can because many people do not have the best material to make very protective face masks.
